Lookaly Closing Down

Lookaly closing down

Sunday October 26th 2014

Dear Lookaly community,

Lookaly will be shutting down on Sunday October 26th 2014. From this date none of the current website, including profile pages and reviews, will be accessible.

James and I started Lookaly in 2008 while at University. Back then we thought it was hard to find the best places in Northern Ireland for things like food, drink, entertainment and shopping. So we set out to change that and created a social review site that enabled people to rate and share their experiences.

Since then there have been many of these types of applications and they are great services. Yelp, TripAdvisor, FourSquare, Google, Facebook. It’s hard, or nearly impossible, to compete with these players. James and I are product people. Our skill set and interest is design and development, user experience and code, so we always struggled to create a sustainable business model for Lookaly.

James and I kept the website running as we followed different career paths, in the hope that one day Lookaly would become the service and business we once envisioned. Unfortunately we’ve realised that this will be unlikely for a while now. It’s now little more than a side project that we struggle to dedicate time to.

At our peak we had 40,000 active visitors per month and even won a DANI award for our iPhone app. We still have a bunch of active users and we’re really grateful and sad that we have to shut down the service for you guys.

We don’t want you to lose the reviews that you put effort into writing or the photos you've uploaded. Just complete this short form and we will provide you with an export of all your reviews and photos. You can keep these, add them to one of the other social review sites you use or not do anything with them, it's up to you.

Creating Lookaly and building up a community was a lot of fun. Thanks for your support throughout the journey.

Lee Munroe, Co-Founder Lookaly
